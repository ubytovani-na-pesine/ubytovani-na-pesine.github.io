Comfortable accommodation within easy reach of the city centre, free parking

Two private ground-floor apartments in a family house located in Dolní Chabry, a charming residential district with excellent access to the city centre. These self-contained apartments with a full kitchen are ideal for guests who value comfort, privacy, and a quiet atmosphere, while still enjoying easy access to the heart of the city.

Free parking is available on the property or directly in front of the house. Public transport connection just around the corner. Metro station 8 minutes by bus, city centre approx. 20 minutes by public transport.

Several restaurants, a café, confectionery, and a supermarket are all within walking distance.
